Nikshay Patra- Hypro App Description

Nikshay Patra- Hypro is a government-backed mobile application under the National Tuberculosis Elimination Program (NTEP) designed to deliver aid directly to patients in need. This app allows authorized users to help provide meals to patients as part of a healthcare initiative aimed at ensuring patients receive timely and proper nutrition.

Features:

  • Food Delivery: Request meals based on dietary preferences and requirements.
  • Geo-Tracking: Real-time location tracking ensures meals are delivered to the correct recipient.
  • Government-Approved: Serving patients under a government initiative to provide food aid.
  • Secure Data Handling: Personal and location data is used solely to ensure proper delivery and is handled securely in compliance with privacy laws.
